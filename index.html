Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What services do you offer?

I specialize in high-performance custom WordPress themes, web design, and development services tailored for agencies and medium to large organizations.

How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline for building a website varies based on complexity, but typically ranges from 4 to 12 weeks. I will provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you provide ongoing support after the website is launched?

Yes, I offer ongoing support and maintenance packages to ensure your website remains up-to-date and secure.

Can you help with SEO for my website?

Absolutely! I implement SEO best practices during the development process and can provide ongoing SEO services to help improve your website's visibility.

What is your process for working with clients?

My process includes an initial consultation to understand your needs, followed by project planning, design, development, and finally, testing and launch. I prioritize communication throughout the project.
